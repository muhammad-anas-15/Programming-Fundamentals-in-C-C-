Exploring Artifical Intellegence

What is Artifical Intelligence?

Artifical Intellegence is the simulation of human intellegence in machines that are programmed to think and
learn like humans.

Key Components of AI

  • Macine Learning
  • Natural language processing(NLP)
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics

Applications of AI

  • Virtual Assistants(eg., Siri,Alexa)
  • Recommendation Systems
  • Autonomus Vehicles
  • Healthcare Dignostics

Challenges and Ethical Considerations

While AI offers many benefits, there are challenges such as bias in alogritms, job displacemet, and ethical
concerns regarding privacy and decision-making.
